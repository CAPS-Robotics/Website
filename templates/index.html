CAPS App Camps

Participate in a summer mobile app development camp where you will experience computer science, engineering and software development through the creation of mobile applications.

  • Students will work in pairs and as individuals to learn the basics of app development
  • They will have the opportunity to make their own application from scratch
  • This is an entry level camp and no prior programming experience is necessary
  • Laptops and mobile devices will be provided for daily use

These 4-day camps are for students who will be in 7th, 8th or 9th grades during the 2018-19 school year. The camps are identical so DO NOT sign-up for multiple sessions.

Camps are from 8:30 AM – 3:00 PM. Campers must bring a lunch. The cost is $185 and proceeds go to support Team 2410 FRC Robotics program.
No refunds. Non-transferable.

Register for Session 1: June 4–8

Register for Session 2: June 18-21


FIRST Robotics Team 2410, The Metal Mustangs, is excited to announce “Girls Going Places,” the Girls Engineering Camp to be hosted at the Blue Valley School District CAPS facility. Each day focuses on two different types of engineering (from electrical to biological to aeronautical) and corresponding activities. Campers will also have the opportunity to work throughout the week on an open-ended Capstone project, which will challenge girls to create solutions to real problems in our community. The camp will end with a presentation of the final project. All proceeds go to supporting the Blue Valley CAPS Robotics Team FRC 2410.

Additional Information:

  • Students will bring a sack lunch
  • Closed-toe shoes, hair tied back, no loose jewelry
  • Drop-off and pick-up must be no earlier/later than 15 minutes of scheduled camp time
  • Paid registrations are non-refundable/non-transferable.
