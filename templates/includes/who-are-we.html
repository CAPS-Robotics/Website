We are FRC Team 2410, The Metal Mustangs, from the Blue Valley School District in Overland Park, Kansas. Our robotics team competes in FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) Robotics Competitions. In FRC, high school students world wide build a robot in a 6-week time span for the year's competition. We are dedicated to providing oppourtunities for high school students to explore applications of science and technology as well as achieve personal growth and learning.
