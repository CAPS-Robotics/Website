Female members of CAPS Metal Mustang Robotics, FRC 2410, had the opportunity to exhibit at the TEDxKC Women's event held in partnership with the Kauffman Foundation. TEDxKC Women highlighted influential women leading local and international organizations working to solve important community issues. As the youngest exhibitors and the only robotics team invited to the event, the Metal Mustangs represented FIRST Robotics in promoting young women in STEM. Team members met with inspirational speakers from the KC area as well as networked with industry leaders.
